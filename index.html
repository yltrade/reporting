The Dominant Reporting Structure

TSIA benchmark data reveals a clear and dominant trend in the technology industry. The vast majority of Education Services organizations are structured under a single, centralized umbrella to ensure consistency and global reach.

74%

of Education Services organizations report into a Global Services Organization.

This centralized model is preferred over regional leadership to standardize content, streamline delivery, and create a unified learning experience for customers worldwide.

Common vs. Uncommon Alignments

While a Global Services org is the most common home, ES often resides within other service-focused departments. Reporting to Product or Sales, however, is notably rare.
